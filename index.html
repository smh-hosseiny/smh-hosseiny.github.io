About Me

Hello, I'm Seyed Mohamad Hossein Hosseini. I'm a graduate student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Elder Lab at YorkU. I completed my B.Sc. in Electrical Engineering from the University of Tehran in 2021. I was a member of the Computational Audio-Vision Lab, where I worked on 3D reconstruction of symmetric objects from single images under the supervision of Prof. Reshad Hosseini.

My research interests include machine learning, computer vision, and medical image analysis. You can find my CV here.
